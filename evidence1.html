Evidence Set 1 – Year 9 Portfolio Assessment (Food Security)

Weekly portfolio-based assessment structure for a Year 9 Food Security unit, showing ongoing monitoring, engagement, and standards alignment.

Year 9 Portfolio Assessment (Food Security)

Std 33.13.2 3.33.53.7 Std 55.15.2

Summary / Explanation:

Below is the summary of how this Evidence Set meets relevant standards/phases. Full evidence (lesson plans, tracking sheets, student work, grade data) is available in the shared Drive folder.

Claim: I implemented a weekly portfolio-based assessment for the Food Security unit to improve student engagement, accountability and continuous assessment over the term.

Evidence Overview: The “Standards Meet” document summarises how student tasks, weekly checkpoints and final grades align with the required Standards and Focus Areas.

Context: Year 9 Geography — mixed ability group. The Food Security unit content was broken into manageable weekly tasks rather than a single summative assessment to support student learning and provide ongoing feedback.

Evaluation / Reflection: The approach increased participation and task completion. It provided structured monitoring of student work and allowed early intervention for students who fell behind. For future use, I plan to refine the rubric, include more formative feedback, and engage students in self-reflection of their learning at each checkpoint.
