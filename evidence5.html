Evidence Set 5 – ICT & CoSpaces: WW2 “Choose Your Own Adventure”

Interactive digital storytelling using CoSpaces and Inklewriter to engage students with World War II content and decision-making.

ICT & CoSpaces: WW2 “Choose Your Own Adventure”

Std 22.12.6 Std 33.43.6 Std 44.5 Std 66.26.4

Claim: I designed and implemented a digital “choose your own adventure” story set in World War II, using Inklewriter and CoSpaces Edu, to create an immersive way for students to explore historical content and develop decision-making, empathy and critical thinking.

Evidence: This evidence set includes the CoSpaces Edu completion certificate, my CoSpaces evidence/planning document, and the live “Choose Your Own Adventure – WW2” story. Together, these show my use of ICT tools to design interactive learning and my engagement in professional learning to improve practice.

Context: The resource was developed to support teaching of WWII content in HASS/History. The interactive story allows students to follow different narrative paths, making choices from the perspective of people living through the war. Reflection on the task indicates that this resource is particularly well suited to Year 7–8 students, where the level of complexity and interactivity aligns strongly with their developmental stage.

Evaluate / Reflection: Creating this digital story demonstrates my commitment to using technology to engage students with complex historical events in a more personal, narrative-driven way. The format encourages students to consider consequences of choices and perspectives of different people in wartime. In future, I would gather structured student feedback, add comprehension checkpoints (e.g. short reflection prompts or quizzes embedded after key choices), and further adapt the difficulty and language for specific year levels.

How this meets the Standards:

  • Std 2.1 – Content and teaching strategies: Uses narrative and interactive storytelling as an engaging strategy to teach WWII content, making complex events accessible and meaningful.
  • Std 2.6 – ICT: Integrates CoSpaces and Inklewriter as ICT tools to enhance learning and create a multimodal experience, rather than using technology as an add-on.
  • Std 3.4 – Select and use resources: Selects appropriate, innovative digital resources that match student interests and support understanding of historical context.
  • Std 3.6 – Evaluate and improve teaching programs: Uses reflection and potential student feedback on the digital story to refine how and when this resource is used (e.g. better fit for Year 7–8).
  • Std 4.5 – Use ICT safely, responsibly and ethically: CoSpaces and Inklewriter are used within a monitored, educational context, with teacher oversight of access and use.
  • Std 6.2 – Engage in professional learning: Completion of the CoSpaces course and application of those skills shows engagement in professional learning to expand ICT capability.
  • Std 6.4 – Apply professional learning to improve student learning: Skills from the CoSpaces course are applied through the WW2 interactive story to enhance student engagement and understanding.
