Evidence Set 2 – Parent Communication & Classroom Engagement

Parent–teacher communication and a participation rubric to support student accountability and engagement in Year 10 HASS.

Parent Communication & Classroom Engagement (Year 10 HASS)

Std 4 — 4.1 Std 4 — 4.2 Std 5 — 5.2 Std 5 — 5.5 Std 7 — 7.3

Claim: I used timely, respectful and proactive communication with parents to address inconsistent participation and incomplete work in Year 10 HASS. This communication, combined with a participation rubric, aimed to clarify expectations and foster accountability and engagement.

Evidence Overview: Evidence includes parent-teacher emails (initial communication and parent response), the Year 10 HASS Participation Rubric, and a summary document aligning this evidence to the relevant Standards (Evidence Set 2 – Standards Meet).

Context: Several students in Year 10 HASS displayed low engagement, incomplete tasks and off-task behaviour. To address this, I reached out to parents and caregivers to discuss concerns and expectations, using the participation rubric to clarify acceptable levels of engagement and completion. The aim was to involve parents, increase clarity, and support students in meeting classroom standards.

Evaluate / Reflection: This approach improved clarity around expectations and increased student accountability. The participation rubric and regular communication established clear boundaries. Parents responded positively, reinforcing the importance of home–school collaboration. In future, I plan to use this communication proactively at the start of a unit, and apply the rubric consistently across classes.

Standard Alignment:

  • Std 4.1 / 4.2: Communication and structured expectations supported a safe, inclusive and orderly learning environment.
  • Std 5.2 / 5.5: Feedback to parents and students about participation and engagement demonstrated transparent reporting of learning behaviours.
  • Std 7.3: Engaging parents/carers in student progress and behaviour showed professional collaboration and communication beyond the classroom.
