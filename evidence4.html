Evidence Set 4 – Engagement Rubric & Parent Communication

Participation rubric, classroom expectations, and family communication supporting orderly, accountable learning environments.

Engagement Rubric & Parent Communication

Std 44.14.2 Std 55.25.5 Std 77.3

Claim: I communicated clear engagement expectations and assessment criteria via a participation rubric and parent emails, to support consistent behaviour, accountability and collaboration during lessons.

Evidence Overview: The rubric, parent emails and documentation of communication provide evidence of transparent expectations and parent involvement in student engagement and behaviour management.

Context: Used in classes with inconsistent participation and technology-management issues. Communication and structured engagement criteria helped reinforce expectations and supported students and parents alike to maintain classroom standards.

Evaluate / Reflection: The approach increased student engagement and accountability. The clear communication and rubric helped build a supportive classroom environment. In future, I plan to embed engagement expectations at the start of units and use digital tracking for behaviour and task completion.
